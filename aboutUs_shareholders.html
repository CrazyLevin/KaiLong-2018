SHAREHOLDERS

KaiLong was founded in 2004, with founding shareholders included Citibank, Starwood Capital, Cargill, Rockpoint, PAG Real Estate, Mr. Stephen A. Roth and Mr. Hei Ming Cheng. After a few restructurings, KaiLong’s current shareholders are the management team, D&J China, Warburg Pincus and PAG Real Estate.

  • Management Team

    Majority share is held by KaiLong group management team.

  • D&J CHINA

    D&J China is headquartered in Shanghai and was Co-founded by Warburg Pincus Investment Group (“Warburg Pincus”) in 2014 and headquartered in Shanghai, Dongjiu (the “Company”) is a leading integrated industrial properties and service platform provider in China. D&J’s mandate is to grow into the leading industrial real estate developer in investment, operation and management of standard workshop, headquarter park, built-to-suit workshop and data center, usually a focus on green-field development projects or built-to-suit opportunities with pre-lease commitments.

  • WARBURG PINCUS

    Warburg Pincus is a private equity firm which was established in 1966. Warburg Pincus invests across geographies and sectors at all stages of a company’s life-cycle from its diversified global private equity funds.

  • PAG REAL ESTATE

    PAG’s real estate strategy manages a series of real estate opportunity and core plus funds investing in properties in Japan, China and throughout Asia-Pacific.
