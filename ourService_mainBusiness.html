  • KaiLong Core Fund

    In the end of 2016, Kailong had launched the first RMB-denominated Core Fund targeting income generating buildings with an initial AUM of RMB 2.2 billion and target AUM of over Rmb15 billion. The first batch of AUM comprises 7 stabilized business park and office projects.

  • KaiLong Commercial Real Estate RMB Fund IV (Exited)…

    In December of 2012, KaiLong partnered with AVIC Trust and Bank of China to raise and deploy KaiLong’s fourth RMB fund within a record-breaking six-week period. The fund invested in two commercial buildings totaling 350,000 sq. meters in the Zhangjiang InnoPark area, a prime business location known as the “Silicon Valley” of Shanghai. The buildings have been fully leased separately by two well-known MNCs.

  • KaiLong Commercial Real Estate RMB Fund III (Partia…

    At the end of 2012, KaiLong joined hands with Standard Chartered Bank and Hwabao Trust, establishing a WFOE to invest in two office buildings in Shanghai’s Jinqiao Economic and Technological Development Zone. Domestic RMB fundraising was completed by Hwabao Trust and China Merchant Bank in three phases.

  • KaiLong Commercial Real Estate RMB Fund II (Exited)…

    In 2011, KaiLong collaborated with HwaBao Trust and Bank of China to raise KaiLong’s second RMB fund, targeting commercial properties with stable cash flow, well-designed facilities, and attractive leasing potential in China’s first-and second-tier cities. The fund has invested in five commercial properties located in Shanghai and Chengdu.

  • KaiLong Commercial Real Estate RMB Fund I (Exited)

    Established in 2010, KaiLong’s first RMB fund targeted well-known commercial properties in China’s tier-one cities, investing in the SVT4 and IBM Research Center properties located in Beijing’s Zhongguancun. Under our asset management expertise, the assets produced rental increments of 81% and 76% respectively over three years, and asset value increases of 69% and 57%. The fund was successfully exited in October 2013.

  • KaiLong Greater China Real Estate Fund II (Investment Period)

    In February 2018, KaiLong launched its second USD denominated fund targeting value-add and opportunistic commercial projects in mainland China and Hong Kong. The fund had raised USD500 million and is on track to achieve its hard cap of USD550 million. It had since invested into three projects across China and Hong Kong.

  • Outbound Investment Fund I

    In January 2015, KaiLong established its first Outbound Investment Fund and raised GBP 13 million. The fund included an office building in London with a total GFA of 5,397 square meters.

  • KaiLong Greater China Real Estate Fund

    KaiLong has successfully launched its maiden US dollar fund with a final close of US$238 million in May 2015. The fund targets for value-added and opportunistic commercial investments in mainland China and Hong Kong.
