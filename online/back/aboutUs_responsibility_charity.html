ABOUT KFOUNDATION

In 2011, KaiLong founded KFoundation together with generous support from KaiLong employees and friends, uniting the KaiLong community under our shared mission to give back to society. We are committed to ensuring that 100% of donations are used on charity projects, with no deduction of administrative costs.Our focus is on assisting needy students and underprivileged children, as well as funding sustainable charity projects. Since its inception, KFoundation has raised over RMB 12 million.

Signature Fund Raising Event

Bid For Love

“Bid For Love” is KFoundation’s signature charity auction event. We have successfully held “Bid For Love” for five consecutive years since 2014. Every year, over 30 pieces of artworks has been donated by KFoundation’s friends to support the charity causes.

Run For Love

Since 2015, KFoundation has successfully held four “Run for Love” fund raising events. Each event attracted more than 60 corporate teams and 500 runners and KFoundation’s friends. The purpose of the event is to promote charity, health, team work and fun.

Charity Projects

Farmer Entrepreneurship Baton Relay

Targeting farming communities of roughly 50 households in Heilongjiang and Guizhou, we provide interest-free loans to help them establish sustainable small businesses and upgrade farming practices.

Children’s Art Center

In 2014, we donated RMB 200,000 toward an art center in Shanghai for orphaned and disabled children. We are proud to support the creative exploration that we believe every child needs. We will continue to support this children’s art center.

Students Sponsorship

To date, we have sponsored some 2,500 students varying from secondary school to university in Guangdong, Guizhou and Henan Province. We also organize volunteers to visit sponsored students 3-4 times every year and welcome more volunteers to join.

Medical Care Sponsorship for Orphans

Together with Shanghai Wei Meng, we provide medical care sponsorship for orphans from Shanghai Baby Home and children with congenital heart disease (CHD) from Heart to Heart Shanghai.

Sponsorship for Children with Special Needs

We provide sponsorship for children with special needs from Ed-ability.

Dream Courts Construction

Together with Nike Sports Camps to design and construct basketball court and soccer field for Grassland Elementary School in Longli County, Guizhou.
