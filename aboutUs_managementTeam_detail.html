FOUNDERS & DIRECTORS

MSc at Imperial College, University of London Advanced Management Program for Executive of the Wharton School, University of Pennsylvania  Member of the Hong Kong Institute of Certified Public Accountants Fellow member of the Association of Chartered Certified Accountants (FCCA)

Bank including Greater China Head of Finance and Risk and Senior Manager, Marketing and Operations. For the past 30 years, Mr. Cheng has involved in a wide variety of activities in Greater China Region covering ground up real estate development, real estate investment, fund management, business consultancy, commercial banking operations, liquidity and interest rate risk management. 

Since KaiLong was founded in 2004, Mr. Cheng has built and led the team as one of the leading players in China's vibrant investment and asset management market. KaiLong is one of the few pioneers which has successfully raised RMB real estate private equity fund. Under Mr. Cheng’s leadership, KaiLong is recognized as one of the most successful and competitive regional real estate investment management firms.

Before joining KaiLong, Mr. Cheng was the General Manager of Shui On Land. He overlooked the development and operations of the Rainbow City, City Apartment, Hangzhou Xi Hu Tiandi as well as the construction and operation management of Shanghai Xintiandi. Prior to that, he was the Greater China Head of Finance and Risk at Standard Chartered Bank.
